A three tier Helpline facility is available in the University to provide information, grievances redressel counseling etc related to Admission and examination.

1. HELPLINE Executives 

University Help line through a call centre is operative from 10Am to 1PM and 1:30 PM to 5 PM  daily except on Sundays and national holidays.  Use the call centre for your queries. The helpline has a grievance registration facility where grievance related to examination, results etc can be registered and get your grievance registration number. Using Grievance registration number, you can track status of your grievances.

2. Student Counselor Facility

University has a student counselor who can be contacted for counseling.  Students and their parents are free to contact student counselor for all matters related to examination and admission. If you are not satisfied with information  provided by our helpline executive or your  grievances are unsolved  or pending , use counselor  facility.  To avail counseling facility, please  call helpline numbers  and ask  for student counselor
 
3. University Officers 

The Controller of Examination (COE) and Deputy Registrar (DR )  and other officers  will be  happy to help  you.   A list of  name of Deans/officers, their contact numbers  and type of information for which they can be contacted  is available on web site. Please click here for University Officers, Head of Departments & Unit incharges  etc.
