Welcome to The MLSU Grievance Registration Center

In order to streamline student grievances and to provide better services to solve the grievances, we utilize a Grievance Registration and Monitoring System. Every grievance registration request is assigned a unique registration number which can be used to track the progress and responses online. For your reference we provide complete archives and history of all your grievance requests. A valid email address and Mobile number is required to submit a grievance.

Please take a print out of the Grievance Registration and keep with you for future reference. You may be required to show the same at the University Help line/Single Window.

Call our helpline No. : 0294-6055631, 0294-6500631

University Help line through a call centre is operative from 10AM to 1PM and 1:30 PM to 5 PM daily except on Sundays and national holidays.
